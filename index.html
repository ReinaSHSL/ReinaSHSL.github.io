75 New Cards, 4 Summons and Tons of Interesting Decisions

Yohane emphasizes a high risk high reward play style. Cards are designed so that every play is a decision that must be weighted against another. Incredibly powerful card effects must be weighed against their negative effects too. On top of that, she has a lower max health and fewer Block cards (but more ways to avoid damage) so every battle is tense. Yohane can summon her iconic Little Demons to help her in the battle to Slay the Spire. New mechanics such as Secrets, Summons and Fallen Energy will let you make interesting decisions and ultimately, Slay the Spire.

Design Philosophy
Yohane is designed to be character with tons of cards interactions that all play off each other to creative explosive heights. Her incredibly high power is inhibited by her inability to control it, leading to dangerous situations if you're not careful. Summons exist to support and augment her ability to pop off in a fiery explosion. Overall, she is a high offense, low defense character where the goal of every card is to make you think if it's worth playing instead of any other card in your hand.
